You awake to the sound of seagulls chirping around you. You find yourself lying on a desolate beach with a sore head, bloody hands and a lingering taste of salt in your mouth. The beach appears small lengthwise but deep. The tall cliffs surrounding you make the cove that you’re in seem isolated. You look around to see your boat dashed into pieces beside you. You also make out that there is a large cave entrance at the top of the cove which looks uninviting…
